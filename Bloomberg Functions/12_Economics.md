# 12 — Economics & Government

Functions for macroeconomic data, central bank monitoring, forecasts, and government policy.

---

## Economic Data

### `ECST`
**Description**: Economic Statistics — search and chart any economic indicator by country (GDP, CPI, unemployment, PMI, etc.).  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Country selector
- Indicator category (national accounts, prices, labor, trade, production)
- Historical data table
- Chart with recession shading
- Seasonal adjustment toggle
- Data release calendar

### `ECOF`
**Description**: Economic Forecasts — consensus and individual economist forecasts for key macro indicators by country.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- GDP, CPI, unemployment, rates forecasts
- Forecast distribution (mean, median, high, low)
- Individual contributor forecasts
- Forecast revision history
- Forecast accuracy tracking

### `ECO`
**Description**: Economic Calendar — upcoming and recent economic data releases globally with expectations, actual, and prior values.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Date range filter
- Country/region filter
- Relevance/impact filter
- Actual vs. consensus (beat/miss)
- Market impact analysis
- Custom calendar

### `ECOW`
**Description**: Economic Workbench — build custom economic dashboards combining multiple indicators and charts.

---

## Country & Sovereign Analysis

### `COUN`
**Description**: Country Overview — comprehensive country profile with economic, demographic, fiscal, and political data.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- GDP and growth data
- Fiscal balance and debt/GDP
- Current account and trade
- Demographics
- Sovereign ratings
- Political structure

### `SOVR`
**Description**: Sovereign Risk Monitor — sovereign credit risk metrics including CDS, ratings, fiscal metrics, and debt sustainability.

### `DEBT`
**Description**: Government Debt Analysis — outstanding government debt by maturity, currency, holder type, and interest cost.

### `BOP`
**Description**: Balance of Payments — current account, capital account, and financial account data by country.

---

## Central Banks

### `WIRP`
**Description**: World Interest Rate Probability — market-implied central bank rate expectations (covered in detail in Money Markets).

### `CENT`
**Description**: Central Bank Monitor — global policy rates, last moves, and forward guidance (covered in Money Markets).

### `FOMC` / `ECBM` / `BOJ` / `BOE`
**Description**: Central bank-specific monitors with meeting dates, statements, projections, and voting records.

### `DOTS`
**Description**: Fed Dot Plot — FOMC individual rate projections mapped over time.

### `SEP`
**Description**: Summary of Economic Projections — Fed's quarterly projections for GDP, unemployment, inflation, and rates.

---

## Global Trade & Geopolitics

### `TRAD`
**Description**: Trade Flow — bilateral trade data by country pair, product category, and time period.

### `ECTR`
**Description**: Economic Trade — trade statistics dashboard with exports, imports, balance, and partner analysis.

### `BMAP` (Geopolitical)
**Description**: Bloomberg Map — geospatial data visualization for economic and political data (sanctions, trade routes, conflict zones).

### `SANC`
**Description**: Sanctions Monitor — sanctions data (OFAC, EU, UN) with entity search and compliance screening.

---

## Regional & Thematic

### `BNEF`
**Description**: Bloomberg NEF — new energy finance research covering clean energy, transport, digital industry, and commodities.

### `ESG`
**Description**: ESG Scores — Environmental, Social, and Governance scores and data by company.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Bloomberg ESG disclosure score
- Third-party ESG ratings (MSCI, Sustainalytics)
- Carbon emissions data
- Governance metrics
- Controversy monitoring
- Peer comparison
