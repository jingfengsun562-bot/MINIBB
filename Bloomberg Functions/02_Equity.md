# 02 — Equity

Functions for stock analysis, screening, fundamentals, technicals, and equity market data.

---

## Screening & Discovery

### `EQS`
**Description**: Equity Screening — build custom screens with 4,000+ criteria across fundamentals, technicals, estimates, ownership, ESG, and custom fields.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Criteria builder (drag-and-drop logic, AND/OR)
- Pre-built screens (Value, Growth, Momentum, Dividend, etc.)
- Results table with sortable columns
- Back-test screen criteria over time
- Export to Excel or PORT

### `RSSL`
**Description**: Relative Stock Screener — screen stocks relative to peers, sector, or index on fundamental and technical criteria.

### `MOST`
**Description**: Most Active — real-time display of most active, top gainers, top losers by exchange or region.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Volume leaders
- % gainers / losers
- New highs / lows
- 52-week range leaders
- Filter by exchange, sector, market cap

---

## Fundamental Analysis (Security-Level)

### `DES`
**Description**: Security Description — summary page with key identifiers, sector, exchange, market cap, shares outstanding, and corporate actions.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Identifiers (ISIN, CUSIP, SEDOL, FIGI)
- Corporate profile
- Capital structure summary
- Key dates (ex-dividend, earnings, fiscal year end)

### `FA`
**Description**: Financial Analysis — full financial statements (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow) with historical data, segment breakdowns, and ratios.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Income Statement (annual / quarterly / semi-annual)
- Balance Sheet
- Cash Flow Statement
- Ratios (profitability, leverage, liquidity, efficiency)
- Segment data (by geography and business line)
- Standardized vs. as-reported
- Peer comparison table

### `GF`
**Description**: Graph Financials — chart any financial data item over time (revenue, margins, EPS, etc.).

### `RV`
**Description**: Relative Value — multi-company comparison table of valuation, growth, profitability, and leverage metrics.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Pre-built templates (Valuation, Growth, Profitability)
- Custom column builder
- Peer auto-detection or manual selection
- Scatter plot mode
- Historical comparison

### `DVD`
**Description**: Dividend data — history, yield, payout ratio, ex-dates, frequency, and growth.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Dividend history table
- Yield chart over time
- Payout ratio trends
- Special dividends and stock splits
- Projected dividends

### `DDIS`
**Description**: Debt Distribution — outstanding debt breakdown by maturity, currency, coupon type, and seniority.

### `CACS`
**Description**: Corporate Actions Calendar — upcoming and historical events (splits, dividends, rights issues, spin-offs, mergers).

## Estimates & Earnings

### `ANR`
**Description**: Analyst Recommendations — consensus and individual broker ratings (Buy/Hold/Sell), target prices, and historical changes.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Consensus summary (mean, median, high, low)
- Individual broker detail
- Target price history chart
- Rating change timeline
- Recommendation distribution (pie chart)

### `ERN`
**Description**: Earnings Analysis — EPS estimates, surprises, revision trends, and earnings quality.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- EPS estimates (annual / quarterly)
- Surprise history (beat/miss)
- Estimate revision momentum
- Earnings quality score

### `EE`
**Description**: Earnings Estimates — detailed consensus estimates for revenue, EPS, EBITDA, and other items across brokers.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Revenue / EPS / EBITDA / FCF estimates
- Broker-by-broker detail
- Revision trends (FY1, FY2, FY3)
- Seasonal patterns

### `EM`
**Description**: Earnings Model — Bloomberg's standardized earnings model with line-item estimates.

### `EA`
**Description**: Earnings Calendar — upcoming earnings dates across a watchlist or market.

---

## Valuation

### `GP`
**Description**: Graph Price — interactive price chart with technical overlays, annotations, and comparison securities.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Chart type (line, bar, candle, Heikin Ashi)
- Technical studies (200+ indicators: MA, RSI, MACD, Bollinger, etc.)
- Event overlays (earnings, dividends, splits)
- Comparison securities (overlay multiple tickers)
- Regression and correlation tools

### `FVMB` / `DDM`
**Description**: Fair Value Model Builder / Dividend Discount Model — build DCF, DDM, and comparable valuation models.

### `EQRV`
**Description**: Equity Relative Value — scatter plots and regression of valuation multiples vs. fundamentals across peer groups.

### `COMP`
**Description**: Comparable Analysis — side-by-side comparison of key metrics for selected peers.

---

## Ownership & Supply

### `OWN`
**Description**: Ownership Summary — institutional, insider, and activist ownership breakdown with historical changes.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Top holders (institutional, mutual fund, ETF)
- Insider transactions
- Activist positions
- Ownership concentration
- Historical ownership trends
- Holder type breakdown (pie chart)

### `OMON`
**Description**: Options Monitor — real-time options chain with Greeks, volume, and open interest.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Call/put chains by expiry
- Greeks (delta, gamma, theta, vega)
- Volume and open interest
- Implied volatility per strike
- Unusual activity flags

### `SI`
**Description**: Short Interest — shares sold short, short interest ratio, days to cover, and historical trends.

### `MMAP`
**Description**: Market Map — tree-map visualization of market/sector performance (heat map by market cap and return).

---

## Technicals

### `BTST`
**Description**: Back-Test — back-test trading strategies built from technical indicators with P&L simulation.

### `TECH`
**Description**: Technical Analysis — summary of technical indicators and signals (overbought/oversold, trend strength, support/resistance).

### `TIC`
**Description**: Tick-by-tick trade data — individual trade prints with time, price, size, and exchange.

---

## Deals & Corporate Events

### `MA`
**Description**: M&A Analysis — mergers, acquisitions, and deal search with filters for status, geography, sector, and deal size.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Deal search / screening
- Deal profile (terms, advisors, timeline)
- Premium analysis
- Comparable transactions
- League tables (advisor rankings)

### `IPO`
**Description**: IPO Calendar & Analysis — upcoming and recent IPOs with filing details, pricing, and aftermarket performance.

### `EVTS`
**Description**: Events Calendar — aggregated calendar of earnings, dividends, conferences, economic releases, and corporate actions for a security or portfolio.

---

## Equity Market Data

### `WEI`
**Description**: World Equity Indices — real-time global equity index monitor with performance, valuation, and breadth.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Regional grouping (Americas, EMEA, Asia)
- Performance (1D, 1W, 1M, YTD, 1Y)
- Valuation (P/E, P/B, Div Yield)
- Breadth indicators

### `IMAP`
**Description**: Industry Map — interactive sector/industry hierarchy with drill-down to sub-industries and constituent stocks.

### `MEMB`
**Description**: Index Members — list of constituents for any index with weights and fundamental data.
