# 04 — Foreign Exchange (FX)

Functions for spot FX, forwards, options, cross-currency tools, and macro-linked analysis.

---

## FX Market Overview

### `FXIP`
**Description**: FX Information & Pricing — real-time multi-currency monitor showing spot rates, daily change, and ranges for G10 and EM currencies.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- G10 overview
- EM currencies
- Asia / CEEMEA / LatAm groupings
- Cross rates matrix
- Bid/ask spreads

### `FXC`
**Description**: FX Currency Matrix — cross-rate table for selected currencies. Customizable grid.

### `WCR`
**Description**: World Currency Ranker — rank currencies by performance over custom time periods.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Total return ranking
- Carry-adjusted return
- Volatility-adjusted return
- Custom date range
- Filter by G10 / EM / region

### `FXFM`
**Description**: FX Forecast Monitor — consensus and individual bank forecasts for currency pairs by horizon (1M, 3M, 6M, 12M).

---

## FX Spot & Forwards

### `FRD`
**Description**: Forward Rates — FX forward and swap points for any currency pair by tenor (O/N, 1W, 1M, 3M, 6M, 1Y, etc.).  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Forward points table
- Implied interest rate differential
- Forward curve chart
- Historical forward points

### `FXCA`
**Description**: FX Calculator — convert amounts between currencies at spot or forward rates with settlement date logic.

### `OVML`
**Description**: Option Valuation Model — price FX options with Black-Scholes or local vol models. Supports vanillas, barriers, digitals.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Vanilla call/put pricer
- Exotic structures (barrier, digital, one-touch)
- Greeks display
- Vol surface input (ATM, 25D RR, 25D BF)
- What-if scenarios

---

## FX Volatility

### `FXVS`
**Description**: FX Volatility Surface — implied vol surface by delta and tenor for any currency pair.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- ATM volatility curve by tenor
- Risk reversal (25-delta)
- Butterfly (25-delta)
- Surface heatmap
- Historical comparison

### `FXHV`
**Description**: FX Historical Volatility — realized volatility over rolling windows, compared to implied vol (vol risk premium).

### `HVG`
**Description**: Historical Volatility Graph — chart realized vs. implied volatility over time.

---

## FX Strategy & Analytics

### `FXST`
**Description**: FX Strategy tools — carry trade monitor, PPP fair value, and momentum signals.

### `FXFV`
**Description**: FX Fair Value — purchasing power parity (PPP) and BEER model estimates for currency pair fair value.

### `FXTF`
**Description**: FX Trade Finder — systematic strategy backtester for FX carry, momentum, and mean-reversion strategies.

### `EMFX`
**Description**: Emerging Market FX Monitor — EM-specific dashboard with spot, NDF, forwards, and carry metrics.

---

## NDF & Restricted Currencies

### `NDF`
**Description**: Non-Deliverable Forward pricing for restricted currencies (CNY, INR, KRW, BRL, TWD, etc.).  
**Key sub-screens**:
- NDF bid/ask by tenor
- Onshore vs. offshore spread
- Implied yield differential
- Fixing source and date

### `FXGO`
**Description**: FX Trading Platform — electronic FX trading with streaming prices from liquidity providers. Supports RFQ, streaming, and algo execution.
