# 13 — News & Research

Functions for real-time news, analyst research, regulatory filings, and transcripts.

---

## Real-Time News

### `TOP`
**Description**: Top News — Bloomberg's curated top news headlines across markets, politics, economics, and technology.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Top Stories (global)
- Regional editions (US, Europe, Asia)
- Market-moving stories
- Exclusive / first-on-Bloomberg stories

### `N`
**Description**: News Search — full-text search of the Bloomberg news archive with keyword, date, source, and topic filters.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Keyword search with Boolean logic
- Date range filter
- Source filter (Bloomberg, Dow Jones, Reuters, wires)
- Topic/industry filter
- Security-linked news
- Alert creation from search

### `CN`
**Description**: Company News — all news articles related to a specific loaded security, filtered by relevance.

### `NH`
**Description**: News Headlines — streaming news headline ticker with customizable filters.

### `NLRT`
**Description**: News Alert — create keyword or security-based news alerts delivered to terminal, email, or mobile.

---

## Research & Analyst Reports

### `BRC`
**Description**: Bloomberg Research — aggregated sell-side and independent research reports. Search by company, sector, or analyst.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Report search (company, sector, theme)
- Broker/contributor filter
- Report type (initiation, upgrade/downgrade, industry, macro)
- Analyst profile and track record
- Download PDF

### `ANR`
**Description**: Analyst Recommendations — consensus and broker-level ratings (covered in detail in Equity).

### `SURP`
**Description**: Surprise Monitor — earnings and economic data surprises (actual vs. estimate) with market reaction.

---

## Regulatory Filings

### `FA` (Filings)
**Description**: SEC and global regulatory filings — 10-K, 10-Q, 8-K, proxy, annual reports, prospectuses.

### `ERF`
**Description**: Earnings & Regulatory Filings — direct access to SEC EDGAR filings and equivalents for non-US companies.  
**Key sub-screens**:
- Filing type filter (10-K, 10-Q, 8-K, DEF 14A, S-1)
- Full-text search within filings
- Recent filings feed
- Comparison tool (diff two filings)

### `BLAW`
**Description**: Bloomberg Law — legal research including court dockets, regulatory filings, and transactional data. (Requires separate subscription.)

---

## Transcripts & Events

### `EVTS`
**Description**: Events Calendar — earnings calls, investor days, conferences, and analyst meetings schedule.

### `BI TRAN`
**Description**: Transcripts — full text of earnings calls, investor day presentations, and management commentary. Searchable by keyword.

### `BRIE`
**Description**: Company Briefs — AI-generated summaries of earnings calls, highlighting key themes and guidance changes.

---

## Sentiment & Alternative Data

### `NSTM`
**Description**: News Sentiment Monitor — Bloomberg's NLP-derived sentiment scores from news flow for individual securities.

### `TRND`
**Description**: Trending — trending topics, securities, and themes based on Bloomberg terminal user activity and news volume.

### `RBLX` / `SSTI`
**Description**: Social Sentiment — social media and web-based sentiment indicators for securities.

### `ALT`
**Description**: Alternative Data — catalog of alternative data sets available on Bloomberg (satellite imagery, web traffic, job postings, patent filings, etc.).
